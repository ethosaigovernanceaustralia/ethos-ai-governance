Organisations

Key External Bodies

OAIC

Office of the Australian Information Commissioner - the primary privacy regulator in Australia.

Australian AI Safety Institute

AISI - provides risk assessments and guidance on AI safety for high-risk AI systems. Operational from early 2026.

ISO

International Organization for Standardization - publisher of ISO/IEC 42001:2023 and ISO/IEC 27001.

Standards Australia

The peak non-government standards body in Australia. Adopted AS ISO/IEC 42001:2023 in February 2024.
